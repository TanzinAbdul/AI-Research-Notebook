## Overview

This repository serves as a centralized research notebook documenting the theoretical foundations, mathematical formulations, architectural designs, implementation details, and emerging research directions across modern Artificial Intelligence.

Unlike traditional tutorial repositories, this project focuses on **understanding why modern AI works**, bridging the gap between reading research papers and implementing real-world systems.

The notes are written from a research perspective, emphasizing intuition, mathematical rigor, architectural understanding, and practical implementation.

This repository is continuously updated as new papers, architectures, and research trends emerge.

---

# Objectives

The primary goals of this repository are:

* Build a comprehensive AI knowledge base
* Understand modern deep learning architectures from first principles
* Summarize influential research papers
* Explore mathematical derivations behind AI models
* Implement important algorithms from scratch
* Investigate future research directions
* Document personal research ideas and observations
